About Us

We don't just take photos —
we craft visual poetry

Founded in 2012, Lumière Studio has been at the forefront of fine-art photography in India. Our name, drawn from the French word for light, reflects our philosophy: every photograph is a dance between light and shadow, between the fleeting and the eternal.

Led by acclaimed photographer Arjun Mehta, our team brings over a decade of expertise to every shoot. From intimate pre-wedding sessions to grand celebration events, we approach each project as a unique story waiting to be told.

We believe in authenticity over perfection — in capturing real emotions, genuine laughter, and the quiet moments between the planned ones. Our images don't just document events; they preserve the essence of who you are.
